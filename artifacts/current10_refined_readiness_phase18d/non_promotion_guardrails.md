# Phase18D Non-Promotion Guardrails

Phase18D is validation-only, diagnostic, integration-readiness review, and onboarding-checklist operationalization.

Explicit non-runs and non-goals:

- no actual Layer1 grid runs
- no select-dry-run
- no candidate YAML
- no promotion
- no Layer2
- no Layer3
- no WFO
- no live/paper
- no strategies 11-50
- no economic claims
- no H2 confirmation
- no top-row retuning
- no execution truth changes

Grid-inspect and smoke-style inspection are configuration readiness checks only. They are not alpha evidence and must not be used to rank strategies by R, profit factor, or any economic metric.
