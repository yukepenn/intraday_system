- Analyze region robustness, not top-row rankings.

- Keep H1 and H2 diagnostic-only; H2 carries `missing_minute_slots_total=540` and is not clean confirmation evidence.
- Do not create candidate YAML in Phase17.
- Do not run select-dry-run in Phase17.
- Do not promote candidates in Phase17.
- Do not create Layer2 in Phase17.

Expected Phase17 outputs include a strategy surface status matrix, top-neighborhood summary, isolated-top-row warnings, and an updated improvement backlog for Phase18.
