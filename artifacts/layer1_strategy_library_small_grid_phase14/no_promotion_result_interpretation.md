# Phase 14 Result Interpretation

These results are Layer1 small-grid diagnostic smoke. They are not candidate evidence, not promotion evidence, and not Layer2 inputs yet.

Top rows are not candidates. Poor rows should not be used to tune thresholds in this phase. Performance rows may be reviewed only as plumbing and health diagnostics: signal counts, intent counts, accepted/rejected trades, skip/reject distributions, execution metrics, and artifact quality.

The next step is review of diagnostic results, not automatic focused grid expansion and not promotion.
